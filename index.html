About Me

Hello! I'm Keeley Bond, a purpose-driven Software Engineer with a result-oriented mindset. I recently graduated from Arizona State University's School of Computing and Augmented Intelligence with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, where I honed my skills in various aspects of software engineering.

Throughout my academic journey, I completed three software engineering internships at the University of Phoenix, which provided me with valuable real-world experience. These internships allowed me to work on diverse projects, from creating accessible user interfaces to eliminating obsolete applications and implementing Kafka-based solutions. I've gained proficiency in technologies such as JavaScript, Java, Kafka, AWS, and microservices, as well as experience with Agile methodologies.

My coding journey has been complemented by hands-on experience with various programming languages and frameworks. I've worked extensively with C++, C, Java, and Swift, completing challenging assignments in data structures and algorithms. This strong foundation allows me to approach complex problems with a systematic and efficient mindset.

Beyond my core software engineering skills, I bring a unique blend of experiences. I have six years of leadership in freelance drone and HD videography, including editing with Adobe Premiere. My work has been recognized by clients for creative and cinematic artistic decisions in aerial drone footage, including projects for a boat charter company in Exuma, Bahamas.

I apply my problem-solving skills and attention to detail in restaurant hospitality. My experience in this field has sharpened my communication skills and ability to understand and meet diverse client needs effectively. These skills translate well into collaborating with stakeholders in software development projects, allowing me to bridge the gap between technical requirements and user expectations.

I'm passionate about leveraging technology to solve complex problems and create meaningful impact. My diverse background allows me to approach challenges with a unique perspective, combining technical expertise with creativity and strong interpersonal skills. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and contribute to innovative projects that push the boundaries of what's possible in software engineering.
